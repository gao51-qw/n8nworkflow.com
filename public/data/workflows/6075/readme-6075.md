# Multi-jurisdiction smart contract compliance monitor with automated alerts

> ### **How it works**
- **Regulatory monitoring**: Continuously tracks changes in laws, regulations, and compliance requirements across multiple jurisdictions
- **Contract analysis**: AI-powered review of existing contracts to identify compliance gaps and risks
- **Automated alerts**: Real-time notifications when regulatory changes affect your contracts or business operations
- **Compliance reporting**: Generates audit-ready reports and documentation for regulatory compliance

- **Legal databases**: Connect to legal research platforms (Westlaw, LexisNexis, EUR-Lex)
- **Contract repository**: Integrate with your contract management system or document storage

- **AI legal analysis**: Set up OpenAI or specialized legal AI for contract analysis

- **Audit trail**: Configure logging and documentation systems for compliance records
