# Automated competitor price monitoring with Bright Data & n8n

> # 

## Description

This workflow automatically monitors your competitors' product prices and notifies you of any changes. It helps you stay competitive in the market by providing real-time insights into pricing strategies without manual checking.

## Overview

This workflow automatically tracks your competitors' pricing across their websites and notifies you of any changes. It uses Bright Data to scrape pricing data and can generate reports or send alerts when prices change.

### Tools Used

*   **n8n:** The automation platform that orchestrates the workflow.
*   **Bright Data:** For scraping competitor websites without being blocked.
*   **Spreadsheets/Databases:** For storing and analyzing price data.
*   **Notification Services:** For alerting you to significant price changes.

## How to Install

1.  **Import the Workflow:** Download the `.json` file and import it into your n8n instance.
2.  **Configure Bright Data:** Add your Bright Data credentials to the Bright Data node.
3.  **Set Up Data Storage:** Configure where you want to store the price data.
4.  **Customize:** Add your competitors' URLs and the specific products to monitor.

## Use Cases

*   **E-commerce Businesses:** Stay competitive with real-time price monitoring.
*   **Pricing Analysts:** Automate data collection for pricing strategy.
*   **Retailers:** Adjust your pricing strategy based on market trends.
